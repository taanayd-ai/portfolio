Professional Journey

I'm currently working as a Silicon Design Engineer at AMD, Hyderabad, where I focus on RTL verification flows and developing advanced verification methodologies. My work involves regression analysis, debugging complex testbenches, and implementing SystemVerilog-based verification solutions using industry-standard simulators like Xcelium, Questa, and Riviera.

Academic Excellence

I completed my M.Tech in Microelectronics & VLSI from IIT Gandhinagar with a CPI of 9.06, under the guidance of Prof. Sandip Lashkare. My thesis focused on ESD Protection/Clamping Circuits for Brain-Machine Interfaces, where I achieved groundbreaking results including sub-3Œ© dynamic resistance for neuromodulation system-on-chip applications.

Research & Innovation

My research interests span emerging non-volatile memories, RRAM technology, neuromorphic computing, and analog/mixed-signal IC design. I have published 7+ papers in reputed conferences and journals, including work on ESD protection techniques and RRAM-based in-memory computing platforms. My research has been recognized with the Best Paper Award at ICEE 2025.
